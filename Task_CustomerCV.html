Live Project

Our customers want to have all thier CVs(also called resume) ported from word documents to html.
However they would like to see a prototype of one of the CVs.
Your task is to prepare your CV in *.html and beautify it with *.css as a demonstration for our customers.
At the end of your live task you should present the way you performed your tasks!
